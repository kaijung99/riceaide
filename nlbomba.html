Bomba Rice

Bomba rice is a short-grain variety of rice, primarily cultivated in the eastern parts of Spain. It is commonly used in paella and other dishes in Valencian cuisine.

Eating Quality: Medium

Aroma: Mild

Grain Size: Short

Meal Type: Main Dish

Water-Cup Ratio: 3 cups of water : 1 cup of rice

Cooking Time: 18-25 minutes

Cooking Method:

You can cook this rice variety as you would regular white rice: In a medium, heavy-bottomed pot, saucepan, or rice cooker.
