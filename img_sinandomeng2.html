Harvester's Sinandomeng Rice Sack







Cooking Instructions

  • Wash the rice in cold water. Repeat till water is clear and then drain.
  • Soak the rice in water for 15 minutes. Suggested rice to water ratio: 1 cup rice = 1.5 cup water. Increase of decrease the amount of water for softer or firmer rice.
  • Turn on the rice cooker to cook. When cooked, allow the rice to absorb all the moisture in the rice cooker before opening.
  • Fluff the rice, serve and enjoy!
