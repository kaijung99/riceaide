Hope Special Stoneless Rice

It is a local rice product that is milled in Aurora, Zamboanga del Sur. It is spreading through other portions of Mindanao and Visayas in their grocery stores.

Eating Quality: Soft

Aroma: Mild

Grain Size: Medium

Meal Type: Side Dish

Water-Cup Ratio: 1 1/2 cups of water : 1 cup of rice

Cooking Time: 25-35 minutes
