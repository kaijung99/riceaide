Robinson's Supersavers Intan Rice Sack







Cooking Instructions (English)

  • Measure rice using measuring cup. (one cup of uncooked rice will make three bowls of cooked rice).
  • Pour rice into the rice cooker pan and add proper amount of water. (1 cup rice = 1 cup of water).
  • Place the rice cooker pan into the rice cooker and switch it ON.
  • Once the rice is cooked, the rice cooker automatically switch OFF.
  • Leave rice with cover for 10 more minutes before serving.

Paraan ng Pagluluto (Tagalog)

  • Sukatin ang bigas gamit ang isang tasa. (ang isang tasa ng bigas ay katumbas ng tatlong mangkok ng sinaing).
  • Ibuhos ang bigas sa rice cooker pan at lagyan ng sapat na dami ng tubig. (1 tasang bigas = 1 tasang tubig).
  • Ilagay ang rice cooker pan sa loob ng rice cooker at i-SWITCH ON.
  • Kusang hihinto ang rice cooker kapag luto na ang kanin.
  • Iwanan ng nakatakip sa lutuan ng 10 minuto bago ihain.
